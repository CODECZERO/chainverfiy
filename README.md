**Live App:** [https://chainverfiy1.vercel.app](https://chainverfiy1.vercel.app)

ChainVerify is constantly evolving. I'm writing this not just to showcase a product, but to share my thought process and the journey behind this project with anyone reading.

## The Story Behind My Vision

When I first started ChainVerify, my idea was specific and somewhat rigid: I wanted to create a decentralized, trust-based platform designed specifically to align with global EU supply chain policies. It was a highly technical solution to a bureaucratic problem. 

But as I listened to early users and tackled real-time issues, my perspective fundamentally shifted. I realized that the core problem of trust wasn't just about corporate compliance—it was about everyday people. 

Have you ever ordered a parcel from Amazon and noticed that little QR-coded, tamper-evident sticker they use to verify the product's authenticity? The moment I saw that sticker on my own package, something clicked. 

*(Here are the images of the Amazon parcel verification that inspired me:)*  
![Amazon QR Code Verification 1](./assets/amazon-qr-1.jpg)  
![Amazon QR Code Verification 2](./assets/amazon-qr-2.jpg)

I realized I could build something just like that, but far more accessible, completely decentralized, and undeniably secure using the **Stellar blockchain**.

My vision has evolved from a niche policy-compliance tool into a global movement for *anybody, anywhere*. 

My ultimate goal is deeply personal: I want to scale this idea so that anyone—whether you're a local artisan halfway across the world or a large distributor—can quickly sell and verify products across time and regions without friction. I want to put the power of absolute, unbreakable trust into the hands of everyone.

## Scaling Through Targeted Feedback

To ensure this next phase isn't just an idealistic vision, I am actively broadening my user base and gathering real-world feedback. My immediate, actionable goal is to onboard a diverse group of users—from individual sellers to small business owners—and use their direct experiences to shape the platform's features. I am tracking this growth and feedback through a structured, transparent process:

1. **Google Form Collection:** I use a Google Form to collect crucial user details including:
   - Wallet Address
   - Email Address
   - Name
   - Product Feedback (Rate your product experience)
2. **Data Export & Analysis:** All responses are exported into an Excel sheet for thorough analysis and record-keeping.
